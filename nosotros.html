OUR STORY

Born from a simple idea in Bahía Blanca.

FROM BAHÍA BLANCA TO MIAMI

Real butcher know-how, brought to the USA.

Total Carnes started as a simple idea in Bahía Blanca, Argentina: a butcher shop run on real product knowledge, where the person behind the counter actually knows the cut you're buying.

In 2022, that idea crossed into the U.S. market. What began as a single butcher counter grew into a broader retail concept — four stores across Miami-Dade and nationwide shipping — without losing the thing that made it work in the first place: trust.

Today, customers come back not because of a discount, but because they know exactly what they're going to get. That's the whole model: quality and consistency, repeated, order after order.
